Our Global Impact

Through the Indian Principals' Network (IPN), we've connected educational leaders across 55+ countries, fostering international collaboration and knowledge exchange.

Frequently asked questions

Here are the answers to the most common questions about our educational initiatives.

How can I join the Indian Principals' Network?

School leaders can join IPN by visiting our website at ipnfoundation.org and filling out the membership application. We welcome principals, directors, and educational administrators from all types of schools.

What programs does IPN offer for teachers?

IPN offers various professional development programs for teachers, including IPN Mission AIM (training teachers in AI), educational leadership workshops, and specialized skills development courses. Visit ipnacademy.in for more information.

How can schools participate in EduAce quiz programs?

Schools interested in our quiz competitions can register through questeduace.in. We organize quiz competitions across multiple cities, focusing especially on Tier 2 and Tier 3 regions to identify and nurture emerging talent.

Do you offer international collaborations?

Yes, through IPN Global we facilitate international collaborations and knowledge exchange programs for educational institutions. Visit global.ipnfoundation.org to learn about our global initiatives spanning 55+ countries.

How can I request a speaking engagement?

For speaking engagements, quizmaster services, or educational consultations, contact us at gaurava@eduace.in or call +91-084007-00199. Gaurava Yadav speaks on educational leadership, innovation, and the future of learning.

What educational resources do you provide?

Our organizations provide a wide range of educational resources including curriculum materials, professional development workshops, online courses through magicofskills.com, and personalized mentoring programs. We aim to support educators at all levels.
